Meet the Founders

Israel Pulla

Israel grew up in Hampton Bays and knows the pulse of beach-town business firsthand. He is a student at Bentley University pursuing a double major in Business Management and History, along with an MBA in Business Management. With hands-on consulting experience, he understands how to help small businesses identify opportunities, strengthen their competitive edge, and grow sustainably. After seeing so many local businesses struggle to adapt during the summer season, Israel knew there had to be a better way to support them with real, actionable guidance. He cares deeply about his community and created Ocean Avenue Consulting to support the betterment of local businesses and the people behind them.
